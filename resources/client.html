SharedBoard - Help

SharedBoard is a free website that allows people to share virtual boards.
Feel free to join a custom room by pressing R.

You can also see a list of useful commands on the top left side of the screen.

Key bindings

  • Backspace / Delete - clear
  • C / Wheel - color
  • D - download
  • Enter - chat
  • H - help
  • M - mine
  • N - name
  • R - room
  • Right click - white
  • T / Shift + Wheel - tool
